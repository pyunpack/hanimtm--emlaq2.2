Stock Reservation on Sales Flow

Main Features:

  • Allow you to create reservation from Quote.
  • Allow you to unreserve stock once customer confirm order.
  • Email notifiction to Stock users during reservation from Quote.
  • Allow you to choose which product from Quote you want to reserve.
  • Allow you to choose how much quantity of product from Quote you want to reserve.
  • Stock reservation records and pivot.
  • Complete history of stock reservation on sales order.
  • Reservation Stock Location created by system.
  • Unique id of reservation.
  • For more details please see below screenshots and watch video.
